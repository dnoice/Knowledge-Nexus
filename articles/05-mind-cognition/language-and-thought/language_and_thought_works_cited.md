## Citation Notes

### Source Quality

All sources included in this bibliography meet at least one of the following criteria:

- Peer-reviewed academic publication
- Authoritative institutional source (NIH, Stanford, MIT, etc.)
- Established academic encyclopedia (Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy, IEP)
- Reputable science journalism with expert consultation
- Recent (2024-2026) cutting-edge research

### Access Information

- **Open Access**: PMC (PubMed Central) articles are freely accessible
- **Paywalled**: Some Cambridge and Oxford articles require institutional access
- **Preprints**: arXiv and OpenReview sources are pre-publication but peer-reviewed
- **Archive Access**: Stanford Encyclopedia entries available in multiple versions

### Research Methodology

This bibliography was compiled through systematic web search of:

1. Foundational cognitive science literature
2. Recent neuroimaging and neuroscience studies (2024-2026)
3. Cross-linguistic and anthropological field studies
4. Contemporary AI/LLM research
5. Public health and aging research

All sources were verified active as of January 10, 2026.

## Using This Bibliography

### For Academic Research

- Start with Stanford Encyclopedia entries for theoretical grounding
- Consult NIH/PMC sources for empirical evidence
- Review arXiv for cutting-edge computational perspectives

### For Interdisciplinary Study

- Neuroscience: Focus on PMC, Journal of Neuroscience, Oxford Academic
- Linguistics: Cambridge, Stanford Language Lab, Levinson/Boroditsky work
- AI/ML: arXiv, OpenReview, MIT News, industry blogs
- Psychology: NIH studies, developmental comparisons, cognitive aging

### Citation Format

For formal citation, convert URLs to proper academic format (APA, MLA, Chicago) using:

- Author names (where available)
- Publication dates
- DOI numbers (for academic papers)
- Journal titles and volume numbers
